Intensity at position r.

Calculates the intensity at position \(r\), the normalised position on the stellar disk.

\[ I(r) = 1 - \sum_{n=1}^4 c_n (1 - \mu^{n/2}) \]

with \(\mu = \cos \theta = (1-r^2)^{1/2}\).

Parameters:
[in]rNormalised distance
[in]coeffsstd::vector of coefficients, size 5
